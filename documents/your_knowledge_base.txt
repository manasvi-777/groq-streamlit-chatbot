The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction film directed by the Wachowskis. It features a dystopian future where humans are trapped in a simulated reality.

Inception is a 2010 sci-fi thriller by Christopher Nolan. It explores dream manipulation and features Leonardo DiCaprio as a thief who steals secrets from dreams.

The Godfather is a 1972 crime film directed by Francis Ford Coppola, based on the novel by Mario Puzo. It follows the Corleone crime family.
